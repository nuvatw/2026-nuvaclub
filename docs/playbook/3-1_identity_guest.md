# Identity: Guest

> **Section ID:** `identity-guest`
> **Parent:** Identity System
> **Level:** 0 (No Account)

---

## TL;DR

You're just browsing. No account yet. You can look around but can't participate.

---

## What You Can Do

| Feature | Access |
|---------|--------|
| Preview Lv.1 courses | Yes (trailers only) |
| Read forum posts | Yes |
| Check out the shop | Yes |
| Browse Sprint projects | Yes |

---

## What You Can't Do

| Feature | Reason |
|---------|--------|
| Full course access | Need to sign up |
| Post or comment | Need to sign up |
| Upload projects | Need subscription |
| Mentor matching | Need Duo Ticket |

---

## The Guest Experience

As a guest, you can:

1. **Preview Courses** - Watch course trailers to see what you'd learn
2. **Read Forum** - See what the community is discussing
3. **Browse Shop** - Check out plans and pricing
4. **View Projects** - See what others have built in Sprint

---

## Why Sign Up?

| Stay Guest | Become Explorer |
|------------|-----------------|
| Limited previews | Full Lv.1 courses |
| Read-only forum | Vote and comment |
| No dashboard | Personal dashboard |
| No progress tracking | Save your progress |

---

## How to Upgrade

1. Click "Sign Up" in the top right
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Verify your email
4. You're now an Explorer!

**Cost:** Free
**Time:** 2 minutes
