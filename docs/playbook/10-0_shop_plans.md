## How to Subscribe

### New User
1. Sign up for free (become Explorer)
2. Go to Shop → Plans
3. Select Solo Traveler
4. Choose billing frequency
5. Enter payment info
6. Instant access

### Existing Explorer
1. Go to Shop → Plans
2. Select Solo Traveler
3. Choose billing frequency
4. Enter payment info
5. Instant access

## Billing & Cancellation

### Billing
- Charged on subscription date
- Auto-renews unless cancelled
- Receipt sent via email

### Cancellation
- Cancel anytime
- Access continues until period ends
- No partial refunds
- Can resubscribe later

## FAQ

### Can I try before subscribing?
Yes! Explorer is free and includes all Lv.1 courses.

### What if I cancel?
You keep access until your paid period ends.

### Can I switch from monthly to yearly?
Yes, contact support or do it at renewal.

### Do prices include tax?
Prices shown are before applicable taxes.
