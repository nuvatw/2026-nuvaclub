# Test: Level by Level

> **Section ID:** `test-levels`
> **Parent:** Test
> **Purpose:** Detailed level progression system

---

## TL;DR

12 levels, 4 tiers. Each level tests specific AI knowledge. Progress through them in order.

---

## Level Overview

| Level | Questions | Time | Pass Score |
|-------|-----------|------|------------|
| Lv.1-2 | 10 | 10 min | 60% |
| Lv.3-4 | 12 | 12 min | 65% |
| Lv.5-6 | 15 | 15 min | 70% |
| Lv.7-8 | 18 | 20 min | 75% |
| Lv.9-10 | 20 | 25 min | 80% |
| Lv.11-12 | 25 | 30 min | 80% |

---

## Basic Tier (Levels 1-3)

### Level 1: AI Foundations
**Topics:**
- What is AI?
- Types of AI (narrow vs general)
- Common AI applications
- Basic terminology

**Skills Tested:**
- Identify AI vs non-AI
- Define key terms
- Recognize use cases

### Level 2: Prompt Fundamentals
**Topics:**
- What is a prompt?
- Basic prompt structure
- Common mistakes
- Simple optimizations

**Skills Tested:**
- Write basic prompts
- Identify prompt issues
- Apply simple fixes

### Level 3: AI Tools Overview
**Topics:**
- ChatGPT basics
- Claude basics
- Other AI tools
- Choosing the right tool

**Skills Tested:**
- Navigate AI interfaces
- Select appropriate tools
- Compare capabilities

---

## Intermediate Tier (Levels 4-6)

**Skills Tested:**

### Level 5: AI Parameters

**Skills Tested:**

**Skills Tested:**

## How to Progress

1. Complete a course at level X
2. Take the level X test
3. Score above passing threshold
4. Get your badge!
5. Move to level X+1
