# Shop: Events

> **Section ID:** `shop-events`
> **Parent:** Shop
> **Purpose:** Live events and workshops

---

## TL;DR

nuvaClub hosts live events: workshops, meetups, webinars, AMAs. Some free, some paid. Register early - they fill up!

---

## Event Types

| Type | Format | Price |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Workshops** | Hands-on learning | Usually paid |
| **Meetups** | Community hangouts | Usually free |
| **Webinars** | Expert talks | Mixed |
| **AMAs** | Q&A with experts | Usually free |

---

## Workshops

### What They Are
Hands-on learning sessions with practical exercises.

### Examples
- "Build Your First AI Automation"
- "Advanced Prompt Engineering Workshop"
- "AI Tools for Marketers"

### Format
- Live instructor
- Practical exercises
- Q&A session
- Materials provided

### Duration
Typically 2-4 hours

---

## Meetups

### What They Are
Community gatherings for networking and sharing.

### Examples
- Monthly AI Meetup
- Local Chapter Events
- Virtual Coffee Chats

### Format
- Casual networking
- Lightning talks
- Group discussions
- Social time

### Duration
Typically 1-2 hours

---

## Webinars

### What They Are
Expert presentations on specific topics.

### Examples
- "The Future of AI in 2026"
- "Enterprise AI Implementation"
- "Career Paths in AI"

### Format
- Expert presentation
- Slides/demo
- Q&A at end
- Recording available

### Duration
Typically 45-90 minutes

---

## AMAs (Ask Me Anything)

### What They Are
Open Q&A sessions with experts or the founder.

### Examples
- "AMA with the Founder"
- "Ask a Senior Prompt Engineer"
- "Career Q&A with AI Leaders"

### Format
- Open questions
- Live answers
- Community participation
- Usually recorded

### Duration
Typically 30-60 minutes

---

## How to Join

### Finding Events
1. Go to Shop → Events tab
2. Browse upcoming events
3. Filter by type, date, price

### Registering
1. Click on event card
2. Read event details
3. Click "Register"
4. Complete payment (if paid)
5. Receive confirmation email

### Attending
1. Get reminder email before event
2. Click join link at event time
3. Participate!
4. Get recording after (if available)

## After the Event

### Recordings
- Most events are recorded
- Available in your dashboard
- Accessible for 30 days (usually)

### Materials
- Slides shared after
- Resources and links
- Follow-up email with summary

### Community
- Continue discussions in forum
- Connect with attendees
- Share your takeaways

---

## Tips

1. **Register early** - Popular events fill up
2. **Add to calendar** - Don't miss it
3. **Test tech beforehand** - Check audio/video
4. **Come with questions** - Make the most of it
5. **Engage** - Participate in chat/Q&A
