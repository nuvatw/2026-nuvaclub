# Member Dashboard Overview

> **Section ID:** `member-dashboard`
> **Nav Title:** Your Dashboard
> **Icon:** home
> **Access:** Explorer and above

---

## TL;DR

Your dashboard is your **personal control center**. Access it by clicking your avatar in the top-right. Manage profile, track courses, view favorites, adjust settings.

---

## Accessing Your Dashboard

1. Log in to your account
2. Click your avatar (top-right corner)
3. Select "Dashboard" from the dropdown

---

## Dashboard Sections

| Section | What It Does |
|---------|--------------|
| Profile | Your public info and identity level |
| My Courses | Track learning progress |
| Favorites | Saved content for quick access |
| Settings | Account preferences |
| My Space | Mentorship connections (Duo only) |

---

## Quick Tour

### Header Area
- Your avatar and display name
- Identity level badge
- Quick stats (courses, favorites, etc.)

### Navigation Tabs
- Switch between dashboard sections
- Current tab is highlighted
- Mobile: horizontal scroll

### Content Area
- Main content for selected section
- Cards, lists, and progress indicators
- Action buttons where needed

---

## Dashboard by Identity Level

| Level | Available Sections |
|-------|-------------------|
| Explorer | Profile, My Courses, Favorites, Settings |
| Solo Traveler | Same as Explorer |
| Duo Ticket | All above + My Space |
