- Search bar (find posts)

- Sort and filter controls
- Infinite scroll

- Top Contributors leaderboard

## Finding Content

### Search
- Real-time filtering
- Searches titles and content
- Filter by category

### Sort Options
- **Popular** - Most upvoted
- **Latest** - Newest first

### Time Filters
- All time
- Past week
- Past 24 hours

### Category Filters
- All posts
- Discussion
- Question
- Share
- Resource

---

## Community Features

### Voting
- Upvote helpful content
- Downvote unhelpful content
- Your vote affects post visibility

### Bookmarking
- Save posts for later
- Access from your dashboard
- Private (only you see)

### Sharing
- Copy link to clipboard
- Share via social media

---

## Tips

1. **Search first** - Your question may be answered already
2. **Use the right category** - Helps others find your post
3. **Be specific** - Detailed questions get better answers
4. **Engage** - Comment on others' posts too
