> **Section ID:** `identity`
> **Nav Title:** Your Level
> **Icon:** user

---

## TL;DR

Your **identity level** determines what you can access on nuvaClub. Start as Guest, become Explorer (free), upgrade to Solo Traveler for full access, add Duo Tickets for mentorship.

---

## The Identity Ladder

```
Guest → Explorer → Solo Traveler → (+ Duo Ticket)
  ↓        ↓           ↓                ↓
Browse   Free      All Courses      Mentorship
 Only    Lv.1      + Full Forum     + Space Access
```

---

---

## What Each Level Gets

### Guest (No Account)
- Preview Lv.1 courses (like trailers)
- Read forum posts
- Browse the shop
- Check out Sprint projects

### Explorer (Free Account)
- All Lv.1 courses (free!)
- Vote and comment in forum
- Your own dashboard
- Save favorites

### Solo Traveler (Paid Subscription)
- Every single course (Lv.1-10)
- Post in forum
- Upload Sprint projects
- Vote on projects
- Priority support

### Duo Tickets (Add-on)
- Space mentorship matching
- Discord community access
- My Space dashboard
- Level-specific mentor access

---

## Upgrade Path

1. **Guest** → Sign up free → **Explorer**
2. **Explorer** → Subscribe → **Solo Traveler**
3. **Solo Traveler** → Add Duo Ticket → **Go/Run/Fly**
