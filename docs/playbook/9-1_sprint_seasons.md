# Sprint: How It Runs

> **Section ID:** `sprint-seasons`
> **Parent:** Sprint
> **Purpose:** Season and sprint organization

---

## TL;DR

**Seasons** = quarters (3 months). Each season has **3 sprints** (roughly monthly). Themes vary each sprint.

---

## Organization

### Seasons

A season equals one quarter:

| Season | Months | Quarter |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Season 1 | Jan - Mar | Q1 |
| Season 2 | Apr - Jun | Q2 |
| Season 3 | Jul - Sep | Q3 |
| Season 4 | Oct - Dec | Q4 |

### Sprints per Season

Each season contains 3 sprints:

| Season | Sprints | Timeline |
|--------|---------|----------|
| S1 | Sprint 1, 2, 3 | Jan → Mar |
| S2 | Sprint 4, 5, 6 | Apr → Jun |
| S3 | Sprint 7, 8, 9 | Jul → Sep |
| S4 | Sprint 10, 11, 12 | Oct → Dec |

---

## Sprint Timeline

### Typical Sprint Duration: ~4 weeks

| Phase | Duration | What Happens |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| Announcement | Day 1 | Theme revealed |
| Building | Weeks 1-3 | Create your project |
| Submission | Week 3 end | Deadline for submissions |
| Voting | Week 4 | Community votes |
| Results | End of week 4 | Winners announced |

---

## Themes

Each sprint has a unique theme:

### Example Themes
- AI Automation Tools
- Prompt Engineering Showcase
- Data Analysis with AI
- Creative AI Applications
- AI for Productivity

### How Themes Work
- Announced at sprint start
- Projects should relate to theme
- Loosely interpreted is okay
- Creativity encouraged

## Sprint Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | Currently accepting submissions |
| **Voting Open** | Submission closed, voting active |
| **Completed** | Winners announced, archived |
| **Upcoming** | Announced but not started |

---

## Badges

### Sprint Badges
- **Official** - First sprint of season
- **Special** - Themed or sponsored sprint

### Project Badges
- **1st Place** - Gold badge
- **2nd Place** - Silver badge
- **3rd Place** - Bronze badge

---

## Viewing Past Sprints

### Project Library
1. Go to Sprint page
2. Scroll to Project Library
3. Filter by:
   - Season (dropdown)
   - Sprint (dropdown)
   - Sort (views/stars)

### Archive Value
- Learn from past winners
- Get inspiration
- See community standards
- Understand what works
