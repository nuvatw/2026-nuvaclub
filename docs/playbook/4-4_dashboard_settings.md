# Dashboard: Settings

> **Section ID:** `dashboard-settings`
> **Parent:** Member Dashboard
> **Access:** Explorer and above

---

## TL;DR

Customize your nuvaClub experience. Manage account, notifications, display, and privacy settings.

---

## Settings Categories

| Category | What You Control |
|----------|------------------|
| Account | Email, password, security |
| Notifications | Email and push preferences |
| Display | Theme, language, accessibility |
| Privacy | Profile visibility, data |

---

## Account Settings

### Email
- View current email
- Update email address
- Verify email changes

### Password
- Change password
- Password requirements:
  - Minimum 8 characters
  - At least one number
  - At least one special character

### Security
- Two-factor authentication (optional)
- Active sessions
- Sign out from all devices

---

## Notification Settings

### Email Notifications

| Notification | Default | Options |
|--------------|---------|---------|
| Course updates | On | On/Off |
| Forum replies | On | On/Off |
| Sprint deadlines | On | On/Off |
| Marketing | Off | On/Off |

### Push Notifications

| Notification | Default |
|--------------|---------|
| New course releases | On |
| Mentor messages | On |
| Sprint voting open | On |

---

## Display Settings

### Theme
- Light mode
- Dark mode (default)
- System preference

### Language
- English (default)
- More coming soon

### Accessibility
- Reduced motion
- High contrast
- Screen reader optimized

---

## Privacy Settings

### Profile Visibility

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| Profile searchable | Yes/No |
| Show online status | Yes/No |
| Display activity | Public/Private |

### Data Preferences
- Download your data
- Delete account (irreversible)

---

## Managing Subscriptions

### View Current Plan
- See your identity level
- See billing cycle
- View renewal date

### Change Plan
- Upgrade to higher tier
- Switch billing frequency
- Cancel subscription

### Payment Methods
- Add/remove cards
- Update billing info
- View invoice history

---

## Tips

1. **Enable 2FA** - Extra security for your account
2. **Check notifications** - Turn off what you don't need
3. **Download data periodically** - Keep a backup
4. **Review privacy settings** - Know what's visible
