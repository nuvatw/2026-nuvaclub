## Voting Rules

### The Basics
- One vote per project
- Unlimited total votes (vote for many projects)
- Can't vote for your own project
- Votes are anonymous

### Voting Period
- Usually the last 1-2 weeks of each sprint
- Opens after submission deadline
- Closes before results announcement

---

## How to Vote

### Step by Step
1. Go to Sprint page
2. Find projects in "Voting Open" sprint
3. Click on any project
4. Review the project
5. Click "Vote" button
6. Vote is recorded

### What to Consider
- Does it match the theme?
- Is it well executed?
- Is it creative/innovative?
- Does the demo work?
- Is it useful?

---

## How Winners Are Chosen

### Primary Criteria
**Most votes = highest rank**

### Ranking
1. **1st Place** - Most votes
2. **2nd Place** - Second most
3. **3rd Place** - Third most

### Tiebreakers
- Number of views
- Submission time (earlier wins)
- Admin discretion

---

## What Winners Get

### 1st Place
- Featured spot on homepage
- Newsletter feature
- Special badge on project
- Potential prizes (varies)

### 2nd Place
- Highlighted in newsletter
- Silver badge on project
- Community recognition

### 3rd Place
- Bronze badge on project
- Community recognition
- Honorable mention

### All Participants
- Portfolio visibility
- Project in archive
- Community engagement
- Learning experience

## Tips for Getting Votes

### Before Voting Opens
1. Write a great description
2. Make a compelling thumbnail
3. Record a demo video
4. Get your demo working

### During Voting
1. Share on social media
2. Post in the forum
3. Engage with comments
4. Respond to questions

### What Gets Votes
- Practical utility
- Creative approach
- Clean execution
- Working demo
- Clear explanation

---

## Fair Play

### Do
- Share your project
- Ask for honest feedback
- Vote for projects you like
- Encourage others

### Don't
- Buy or trade votes
- Create fake accounts
- Manipulate the system
- Harass other participants

### Violations
- Disqualification
- Account warning
- Potential ban

---

## After Results

### If You Won
- Celebrate!
- Thank voters
- Share the news
- Update your profile

### If You Didn't Win
- Learn from winners
- Get feedback
- Improve for next sprint
- Keep building

### Either Way
- Review what worked
- Note what didn't
- Plan for next time
- Stay engaged
