## Account & Dashboard

### How do I get to my dashboard?
Click your avatar in the top-right corner. That's it!

### I forgot my password. Help!
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Check your email for reset instructions.

### Can I change my email?
Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Update Email. You'll need to verify the new email.

### Can I delete my account?
Yes. Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. This is irreversible.

## Learning & Courses

### What courses can I access for free?
All Lv.1 (Beginner) courses are free with an Explorer account.

### Do courses expire?
Nope. Once you have access, learn at your own pace forever.

### Can I download courses for offline?
Not currently. Courses stream online only.

### How do I resume a course?
Go to Dashboard → My Courses → click "Continue Learning" on any course.

---

## Tests

### What if I fail a test?
Take it again! Unlimited retries. We only save your best score.

### Can I see which answers I got wrong?
You'll see your overall score and areas to improve, but not specific answers.

### Is there a waiting period between retakes?
No. You can retry immediately (though reviewing material first helps).

## Forum

### Why can't I create posts?
Only Solo Traveler and above can create posts. Explorers can comment and vote.

### How do I report someone being rude?
Click the "..." menu on any post or comment → Report → Choose reason → Submit.

### How do I get on the leaderboard?
Earn reputation by getting upvotes on your posts and comments.

---

## Space & Mentorship

### How do I become a Nunu (mentor)?
Apply in the Space section. You'll need to demonstrate expertise and complete verification.

### What's the difference between Go, Run, and Fly?
- Go: Regular Nunus, Discord
- Run: + Certified Nunus, priority
- Fly: + Founder access, career guidance

### Can I be both a Nunu and Vava?
Not at the same time, but you can switch roles later.

## Sprint

### Who can submit to Sprint?
Solo Traveler and above.

### How are winners picked?
Community votes. Most votes = winner.

### Can I edit my submission after submitting?
Yes, until the submission deadline. After that, it's locked.

---

## Billing & Subscriptions

### Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, anytime. Your access continues until the billing period ends.

### Can I upgrade my Duo Ticket?
Yes! Upgrade anytime. You'll be charged the difference.

### Is there a refund policy?
No partial refunds, but you keep access until your paid period ends.

### Can I switch from monthly to yearly?
Yes, at renewal time or contact support.

## Technical

### What browsers are supported?
Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions).

### The video won't play. What do I do?
1. Try refreshing the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try a different browser
4. Clear cache and cookies
5. Contact support if still broken

### My progress isn't saving. Help!
1. Make sure you're logged in
2. Check internet connection
3. Try refreshing
4. Contact support with details
