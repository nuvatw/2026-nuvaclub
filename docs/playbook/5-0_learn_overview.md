# Learn: How It Works

> **Section ID:** `learn`
> **Nav Title:** Learn
> **Icon:** book
> **Page Route:** `/learn`

---

## TL;DR

**Watch video courses** → **Learn AI skills** → **Track your progress**

All courses are organized by difficulty (Lv.1-10). Start at Lv.1, work your way up.

---

## How Learning Works

1. **Browse** - Find a course in the library
2. **Watch** - Stream lessons anytime, anywhere
3. **Progress** - Your spot is saved automatically
4. **Complete** - Finish all lessons to complete the course

---

## Course Structure

```
Course
 └── Chapter 1
      ├── Lesson 1 (video)
      ├── Lesson 2 (video)
      └── Lesson 3 (video)
 └── Chapter 2
      ├── Lesson 4 (video)
      └── ...
```

Each course contains:
- **Chapters** - Grouped learning topics
- **Lessons** - Individual video segments
- **Duration** - Total watch time
- **Level** - Difficulty rating (Lv.1-10)

---

## Finding Courses

### Hero Carousel
Featured courses at the top of the page:
- Auto-rotating highlights
- Click to preview or start

### Categories
Browse by topic:
- AI Fundamentals
- Prompt Engineering
- Automation
- Advanced Topics
- And more...

### Level Filter
Show only courses at your difficulty:
- Beginner (Lv.1-2)
- Intermediate (Lv.3-5)
- Advanced (Lv.6-8)
- Expert (Lv.9-10)

### Search
Find exactly what you need:
- Search by title
- Search by topic
- Search by keyword

---

## Course Cards

Each course card shows:

```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│ [Thumbnail]                 │
│ Lv.3 Badge                  │
│ Course Title                │
│ Instructor • Duration       │
│ Short description...        │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

### Hover Preview
Hover over any card to see:
- Full description
- Chapter list
- "Watch Trailer" button
- "Start Course" button

---

## Video Player

### Controls
- Play/Pause
- Progress scrubber
- Volume control
- Playback speed (0.5x - 2x)
- Fullscreen toggle
- Picture-in-picture

### Resume Playback
Your position is saved automatically. Click "Continue" to resume from exactly where you left off.

---

## Tips

1. **Start with Lv.1** - Build a solid foundation
2. **Complete before moving on** - Don't skip around
3. **Take notes** - Active learning helps retention
4. **Use the forum** - Ask questions when stuck
