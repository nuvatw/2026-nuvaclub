## The Simple Version

### Free Account (Explorer)
You get **all Lv.1 (beginner) courses** for free. That's actually a lot of content!

What Lv.1 includes:
- Introduction to AI
- Basic prompting
- ChatGPT fundamentals
- Getting started guides

### Paid Subscription (Solo Traveler)
Subscribe to unlock **Lv.2-10**. That's the full library.

What you unlock:
- Advanced prompting (Lv.3-4)
- Automation techniques (Lv.5-6)
- Expert strategies (Lv.7-8)
- Master-level content (Lv.9-10)

---

## What Guests See

Guests can preview courses but can't watch full content:

- Course trailers (like movie previews)
- Course descriptions and outlines
- Instructor information
- Duration and level info

**To watch:** Sign up for free as Explorer

---

## What Explorers Get

Free accounts unlock all beginner content:

- Every Lv.1 course in full
- Unlimited rewatching
- Progress tracking
- Favorites and bookmarks
- Resume from where you left

**Locked:** Lv.2-10 courses (upgrade to Solo Traveler)

---

## What Solo Travelers Get

Full subscription unlocks everything:

- All courses (Lv.1-10)
- Full video library
- Priority support
- Offline downloads (coming soon)
- Early access to new courses

---

## How to Upgrade

### Guest → Explorer
1. Click "Sign Up"
2. Create account (free)
3. Instant Lv.1 access

### Explorer → Solo Traveler
1. Go to Shop → Plans
2. Select Solo Traveler
3. Choose monthly or yearly
4. Instant full access

---

## FAQ

### Can I try before subscribing?
Yes! All Lv.1 courses are free. Plus every course has a trailer.

### If I cancel, do I lose progress?
No. Your progress is saved. If you re-subscribe later, it's still there.

### Can I share my account?
No. Accounts are individual. Sharing violates terms of service.
