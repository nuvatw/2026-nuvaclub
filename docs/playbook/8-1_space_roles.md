# Space: Nunu vs Vava

> **Section ID:** `space-roles`
> **Parent:** Space
> **Purpose:** Understanding the two mentorship roles

---

## TL;DR

**Nunu** = Mentor (the guide). **Vava** = Mentee (the learner). You can be one at a time.

---

## The Two Roles

### Nunu (Mentor)

The guide. The helper. The one with experience.

| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Role | Provide guidance and support |
| Requirements | Demonstrated expertise |
| Commitment | Regular check-ins with Vava |
| Reward | Fulfillment, community status |

### Vava (Mentee)

The learner. The one seeking guidance.

| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Role | Learn and grow with guidance |
| Requirements | Duo Ticket subscription |
| Commitment | Active participation |
| Reward | Personalized learning path |

---

## Nunu Levels

Nunus have experience levels:

| Level | Name | Mentee Slots |
|-------|------|--------------|
| N5 | Beginner | 3 |
| N4 | Intermediate | 5 |
| N3 | Senior | 10 |
| N2 | Expert | 30 |
| N1 | Master | 50 |

### How to Level Up
- Successful mentorship history
- Positive feedback from Vavas
- Platform contributions
- Time and experience

---

## Certified Nunu

Extra verified mentors with professional credentials.

### What It Means
- Verified identity
- Proven expertise
- Professional background
- Higher standards

### Access
- **Duo Go:** No access to Certified Nunus
- **Duo Run:** Access to Certified Nunus
- **Duo Fly:** Access to all + Founder

---

## What Vavas Get

From their Nunu:
- 1:1 support and guidance
- Regular check-ins
- Personalized advice
- Progress feedback
- Industry insights

---

## What Nunus Give

To their Vavas:
- Share their experience
- Answer questions
- Review work
- Provide encouragement
- Open doors

---

## Can I Be Both?

### Short Answer
Not at the same time. But you can switch roles later.

### Why Not Both?
- Prevents conflicts of interest
- Ensures focused commitment
- Maintains clear relationships

### Switching Roles
1. Complete current commitments
2. Update your profile
3. Apply for new role
4. Get approved

---

## Choosing Your Role

### Become a Vava if...
- You're learning AI
- You want guidance
- You're early in your journey
- You have a Duo Ticket

### Become a Nunu if...
- You have expertise to share
- You enjoy teaching
- You want to give back
- You can commit time

---

## The Relationship

### What It Is
- Professional mentorship
- Structured guidance
- Goal-oriented learning
- Mutual respect

### What It Isn't
- Personal friendship (unless it evolves)
- Therapy or counseling
- Guaranteed job placement
- Unlimited free consulting
