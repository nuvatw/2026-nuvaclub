## TL;DR

**Take quizzes** → **Pass with 60%+** → **Earn badges** → **Unlock levels**

Tests match the course levels. Finish a Lv.3 course? Take the Lv.3 test.

---

## Why Test?

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Validate** | Prove you actually learned something |
| **Badges** | Show off your level on your profile |
| **Unlock** | Higher levels unlock content recommendations |
| **Track** | See your growth over time |

---

## How Testing Works

1. **Complete a course** at level X
2. **Take the test** for level X
3. **Score above passing** threshold
4. **Earn your badge** for that level
5. **Move to level X+1**

---

## The 12 Levels

Tests are organized into 12 levels across 4 tiers:

| Tier | Levels | Difficulty |
|------|--------|------------|
| Basic | 1-3 | Beginner |
| Intermediate | 4-6 | Growing |
| Advanced | 7-9 | Expert |
| Expert | 10-12 | Master |

---

## Test Structure

### Basic Tier (Levels 1-3)
- **Time:** 5 minutes
- **Questions:** 5-8
- **Types:** True/False, Multiple Choice
- **Pass:** 60%

### Intermediate Tier (Levels 4-6)
- **Time:** 15 minutes
- **Questions:** 10-12
- **Types:** Multiple Choice, Short Answer
- **Pass:** 65%

### Advanced Tier (Levels 7-9)
- **Time:** 30 minutes
- **Questions:** 15-18
- **Types:** Short Answer, Essay
- **Pass:** 70%

### Expert Tier (Levels 10-12)
- **Time:** 60 minutes
- **Questions:** 20-25
- **Types:** All Essay
- **Pass:** 80%

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Complete the course first** - Tests assess course content
2. **Review before testing** - Refresh your memory
3. **Manage your time** - Don't rush, don't stall
4. **Read questions carefully** - Details matter
5. **Answer everything** - No penalty for wrong answers
