# Dashboard: My Space

> **Section ID:** `dashboard-space`
> **Parent:** Member Dashboard
> **Access:** Duo Ticket holders only

---

## TL;DR

Manage your mentorship connections. See active matches, message partners, track your matching posts.

---

## Access Requirements

| Your Level | My Space Access |
|------------|-----------------|
| Guest | No |
| Explorer | No |
| Solo Traveler | No |
| + Duo Ticket | Yes |

---

## My Space Sections

### Active Matches
Your current Nunu/Vava partnerships:
- Partner name and avatar
- Match date
- Connection status
- Quick message button

### Match History
Past mentorship connections:
- Previous partners
- Duration of match
- Notes/feedback

### Matching Posts
Your active matching requests:
- Post status (active/closed)
- Responses received
- Edit or close posts

### Messages
Communication with matches:
- Conversation threads
- Unread indicator
- Send/receive messages

---

## Managing Matches

### View Match Details
1. Click on any match card
2. See partner profile
3. View match history
4. Access messaging

### Send a Message
1. Click "Message" on match card
2. Type your message
3. Click "Send"

### End a Match
1. Open match details
2. Click "End Match"
3. Confirm decision
4. Provide optional feedback

---

## Matching Posts

### Create a Post
1. Click "Create Matching Post"
2. Describe what you're looking for
3. Set availability
4. Publish

### Manage Responses
1. View incoming responses
2. Review responder profiles
3. Accept or decline
4. Start matching process

---

## For Nunus (Mentors)

If you're a Nunu, you'll also see:
- Your Nunu profile
- Mentorship slots (available/filled)
- Incoming match requests
- Your ratings and reviews

---

## For Vavas (Mentees)

If you're a Vava, you'll see:
- Active mentor connections
- Pending match requests
- Session history
- Notes and progress

---

## Tips

1. **Respond promptly** - Good communication builds trust
2. **Be clear about goals** - Help matches succeed
3. **Update availability** - Keep your status current
4. **Give feedback** - Help improve the community
