# Dashboard: Profile

> **Section ID:** `dashboard-profile`
> **Parent:** Member Dashboard
> **Access:** Explorer and above

---

## TL;DR

View and manage your personal information. Your profile shows your identity, stats, and public presence.

---

## Profile Elements

### Display Info
- **Display Name** - Your name shown across the platform
- **Avatar** - Your profile picture
- **Bio** - Tell others about yourself (optional)

### Identity Info
- **Identity Level** - Your current membership tier
- **Identity Badge** - Visual indicator of your level
- **Join Date** - When you became a member

### Stats
- **Courses Completed** - Total finished courses
- **Current Level** - Highest test level passed
- **Forum Activity** - Posts, comments, reputation

---

## Editing Your Profile

### Change Display Name
1. Click "Edit Profile" button
2. Update the name field
3. Click "Save Changes"

### Update Avatar
1. Click on your current avatar
2. Upload a new image (JPG, PNG)
3. Crop if needed
4. Click "Save"

### Add/Edit Bio
1. Click "Edit Profile"
2. Enter your bio (max 200 characters)
3. Click "Save Changes"

---

## Profile Visibility

| Element | Who Can See |
|---------|-------------|
| Display Name | Everyone |
| Avatar | Everyone |
| Bio | Everyone |
| Identity Level | Everyone |
| Join Date | Everyone |
| Course Progress | Only you |

---

## Profile Best Practices

1. **Use a clear photo** - Helps build trust
2. **Keep bio concise** - 1-2 sentences max
3. **Update regularly** - Reflect your current focus
4. **Be authentic** - Real profiles build real connections
