## Requirements

### Before Buying
You must have an active **Solo Traveler** subscription.

### Why?
Duo Tickets are add-ons, not standalone products. The full platform (courses, forum, sprint) comes with Solo Traveler.

### The Complete Package
```
Solo Traveler ($990/mo)
      +
Duo Ticket (Go/Run/Fly)
      =
Full Platform + Mentorship
```

---

## How to Purchase

1. Subscribe to Solo Traveler (if not already)
2. Go to Shop → Duo Tickets
3. Select your tier (Go, Run, or Fly)
4. Choose billing frequency
5. Complete payment
6. Access Space immediately

---

## Upgrading & Downgrading

### Upgrading
- Go → Run: Pay difference
- Go → Fly: Pay difference
- Run → Fly: Pay difference
- Instant access to new tier

### Downgrading
- Effective at next billing cycle
- No partial refunds
- Keep current tier until renewal

---

## Cancelling

### What Happens
- Space access continues until period ends
- Active matches continue until period ends
- Revert to Solo Traveler after

### How to Cancel
1. Go to Settings
2. Navigate to Subscriptions
3. Click "Cancel Duo Ticket"
4. Confirm cancellation

---

## FAQ

### Do I need Solo Traveler first?
Yes. Duo Tickets are add-ons.

### Can I skip Go and get Run?
Yes. Choose whatever tier you want.

### What if I cancel Solo Traveler?
You lose both Solo Traveler and Duo Ticket access.

### Can I switch tickets?
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime.

### Are there yearly discounts?
Yes, save 17% with yearly billing (2 months free).
